## The Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)

He told me **"It doesn't matter what happens at [home], you need to come in to work"** and withdrew all accommodation. I also have ADHD and sleep apnea (both known, never accommodated), and was disciplined for raising legitimate safety concerns.

**NEW (November 2025):** The employer's lawyer sent me an employment contract addressed to **"Dear Rollins:"** - a completely different employee - and a termination letter that I'm 75% sure didn't list specific reasons when I received it 13 days earlier. This destroys their credibility on ALL documentation.

This is family status discrimination with proven accommodation withdrawal, disability discrimination, and wrongful dismissal - with OHS retaliation and document fabrication as evidence of bad faith.
